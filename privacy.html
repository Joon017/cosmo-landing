Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Cosmo Translator provides real-time voice translation for natural conversations. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how to contact us if you have questions.

Information We Collect

  • Account information such as your email address and login details.
  • Usage information related to sessions, subscriptions, purchases, and credit balances.
  • Audio, transcript, and translation request data needed to provide the service.
  • Technical information such as device, app, and diagnostic details used for reliability and support.

How We Use Information

  • To authenticate your account and keep you signed in securely.
  • To deliver translation, playback, and conversation history features.
  • To manage credit usage, subscriptions, purchases, and support requests.
  • To improve reliability, performance, abuse prevention, and security.

Third-Party Services

Cosmo Translator uses trusted service providers to support authentication, infrastructure, payments, and AI translation features. These providers may process data only as needed to operate the service.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to operate the app, maintain account functionality, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our terms.

Your Choices

You may request account-related support by contacting us. You can also stop using the service at any time and request deletion support where applicable.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact getcosmoapp@gmail.com.
