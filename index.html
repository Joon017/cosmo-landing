What makes Cosmo different

Built for real conversations,
not one-off or superficial translations.

Cosmo is designed for ongoing back-and-forth dialogue, with controls and session tools that support more natural spoken conversations across languages.

🎙️

Live Voice Translation

Speak naturally and see each translated turn appear during the conversation, with spoken playback available as the exchange continues.

🤝

Built for Two-Way Dialogue

Cosmo is designed for sustained back-and-forth conversation, so both sides can continue speaking without treating each line like a separate translation task.

⚡

Near-Instant Response

Low-latency translation helps the conversation keep moving, so responses appear quickly enough to support more natural exchanges.

🌎

50+ Languages

Cosmo supports broad multilingual coverage for travel, business, and everyday conversations across a wide range of languages.

📚

Translation History

Translation history is retained during use and can be cleared or exported when needed.

🎧

Voice Selection

Choose the voice that best fits the conversation and adjust how translated audio is spoken back.

Environmental Noise Setting

Set the current environment noise level so Cosmo can better match quieter rooms or busier public spaces.

interactions and conversations

⚡ Recommended

Hands-Free Mode

Always listening. Cosmo detects when either person speaks and translates automatically — The closest experience to a real interpreter.

  • Automatic voice activity detection
  • Automatic speaker switching
  • Powered by advanced AI speech models
  • Mic sensitivity control
  • Adjustable playback speed
  • Different voices available
🎤 Precise

Press & Hold Mode

Hold the mic button while you speak, then release to translate. Perfect for noisy environments, precise turn-taking, and more controlled conversations.

  • Mic sensitivity control
  • Works great in loud environments
  • Adjustable playback speed
  • Different voices available
